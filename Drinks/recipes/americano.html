Espresso BasedClassic Black

Americano

Smooth and mellow, made by adding hot water to espresso for a lighter yet full-bodied cup. Simple, honest, and endlessly satisfying.

Serving · Hot

Americano

3 Steps Easy Hot Serve 5 min

Tasting Notes

Bold Smooth Earthy Full-Bodied Clean Finish

Brew Specs

Method

Espresso + Hot Water

Espresso

Double Shot

Espresso Beans

18g

Filtered Water (Shot)

36ml

Hot Water

120ml at 90°C

Brew Time

5 min

Brew Recipe

01
Pull a Double Espresso ShotGrind 18g of beans fine, tamp evenly, and extract 36ml in 25–30 seconds.
02
Heat the WaterBring 120ml of filtered water to just below boiling — around 90°C. Do not use a full boil.
03
Combine and ServeAdd hot water to a pre-warmed mug first, then pour the espresso shot on top to preserve the crema.
