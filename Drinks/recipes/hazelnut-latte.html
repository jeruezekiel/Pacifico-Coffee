Hazelnut Latte

Smooth, nutty, and gently sweet — hazelnut syrup brings a toasty warmth to this classic iced latte. Effortless to make, impossible to put down.

Serving · Iced

Hazelnut Latte

4 Steps Easy Cold Serve 5 min

Tasting Notes

Nutty Sweet Toasty Smooth Creamy Finish

Brew Specs

Method

Espresso + Cold Milk

Espresso

Double Shot

Espresso Beans

18g

Filtered Water (Shot)

36ml

Whole Milk

200ml, cold

Hazelnut Syrup

2 Pumps

Brew Recipe

01
Prepare the GlassAdd 2 pumps of hazelnut syrup to the bottom of a tall glass, then fill generously with ice.
02
Add the MilkPour 200ml of cold whole milk over the ice, leaving about 4cm of space at the top for the espresso.
03
Pull the EspressoGrind 18g of beans fine, tamp evenly, and extract a fresh 36ml double shot in 25–30 seconds.
04
Pour and ServeGently pour the hot espresso over the back of a spoon to float it on top of the milk. Stir once and serve immediately.
