Milk-BasedChocolate

Mocha

A rich meeting of espresso, steamed milk, and deep cocoa. Indulgent and warming, with just enough bitterness to keep it honest.

Serving · Hot or Iced

Mocha

5 Steps Easy Hot or Iced 8 min

Tasting Notes

Chocolatey Rich Bittersweet Creamy Indulgent

Brew Specs

Method

Espresso + Steamed Milk

Espresso

Double Shot

Espresso Beans

18g

Filtered Water (Shot)

36ml

Whole Milk

150ml at 60–65°C

Chocolate Syrup

2 tbsp

Brew Recipe

01
Add the ChocolateSpoon 2 tbsp of chocolate syrup or cocoa powder into the bottom of a pre-warmed mug or glass.
02
Pull the EspressoGrind 18g of beans fine, tamp evenly, and extract 36ml directly over the chocolate. Stir to form a smooth, glossy paste.
03
Steam the MilkSteam 150ml of whole milk to 60–65°C with a light, creamy foam. For iced, skip steaming and use cold milk over ice instead.
04
CombinePour the steamed milk steadily over the espresso-chocolate base. For iced, fill the glass with ice before pouring.
05
Finish and ServeTop with whipped cream and an optional drizzle of chocolate syrup. Serve immediately.
