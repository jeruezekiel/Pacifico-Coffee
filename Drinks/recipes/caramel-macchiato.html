Caramel Macchiato

Sweet and beautifully layered — vanilla milk, espresso, and a generous caramel drizzle over ice.

Serving · Iced

Caramel Macchiato

5 Steps Easy Cold Serve 8 min

Tasting Notes

Sweet Caramel Vanilla Rich Layered

Brew Specs

Method

Espresso + Cold Milk

Espresso

Double Shot

Espresso Beans

18g

Filtered Water (Shot)

36ml

Whole Milk

200ml, chilled

Vanilla Syrup

2 pumps (~15ml)

Caramel Sauce

1 tbsp, for drizzle

Brew Time

8 min

Brew Recipe

01
Add Vanilla and MilkPour 2 pumps of vanilla syrup into a tall glass, then add 200ml of chilled whole milk.
02
Add IceFill the glass generously with ice cubes, leaving 4–5 cm of space at the top.
03
Pull a Double Espresso ShotGrind 18g of beans fine, tamp evenly, and extract 36ml in 25–30 seconds.
04
Layer the EspressoSlowly pour the espresso over the back of a spoon held just above the ice so it layers beautifully over the milk.
05
Drizzle and ServeFinish with a generous drizzle of caramel sauce over the top and serve immediately.
