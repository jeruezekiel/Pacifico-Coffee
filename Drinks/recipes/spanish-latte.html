Spanish Latte

Rich and creamy with a natural sweetness from condensed milk layered beneath bold espresso and cold milk. Sweet, smooth, and deeply satisfying.

Serving · Iced

Spanish Latte

4 Steps Easy Cold Serve 5 min

Tasting Notes

Sweet Rich Creamy Bold Smooth Finish

Brew Specs

Method

Espresso + Cold Milk

Espresso

Double Shot

Espresso Beans

18g

Filtered Water (Shot)

36ml

Whole Milk

150ml, cold

Condensed Milk

2 tbsp

Brew Recipe

01
Layer the Condensed MilkSpoon 2 tbsp of sweetened condensed milk into the bottom of a tall glass. This forms the sweet base that slowly blends as you drink.
02
Add Ice and MilkFill the glass with ice, then pour 150ml of cold whole milk over it, leaving space at the top for the espresso.
03
Pull the EspressoGrind 18g of beans fine, tamp evenly, and extract a fresh 36ml double shot in 25–30 seconds.
04
Pour and ServeSlowly pour the espresso over the back of a spoon to create a layered look. Stir gently before drinking and serve immediately.
