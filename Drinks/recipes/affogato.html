Italian ClassicDessert Coffee

Affogato

A simple Italian dessert coffee. A hot espresso shot poured over cold, creamy vanilla ice cream. Two ingredients, one perfect contrast of hot and cold, bitter and sweet.

Serving · Cold

Affogato

3 Steps Easy Cold Serve 3 min

Tasting Notes

Espresso Vanilla Creamy Bittersweet Indulgent

Brew Specs

Method

Espresso over Ice Cream

Espresso

Double Shot

Espresso Beans

18g

Filtered Water

36ml for shot

Vanilla Ice Cream

1–2 scoops

Brew Time

3 min

Brew Recipe

01
Scoop the Ice CreamPlace 1–2 generous scoops of vanilla ice cream into a chilled glass or bowl. Keep it cold until ready to serve.
02
Pull the Espresso ShotExtract a fresh, hot 36ml double espresso immediately before serving. Timing is everything — do not let it cool.
03
Pour and Serve at OncePour the hot espresso directly over the ice cream. Serve immediately while the contrast between hot and cold remains.
