Brown Sugar Espresso

Sweet, bold, and smooth — espresso shaken with brown sugar syrup and poured over oat milk for a caramel-like finish.

Serving · Cold

Brown Sugar Espresso

5 Steps Easy Cold Serve 8 min

Tasting Notes

Brown Sugar Caramel Oat Milk Bold Espresso Cinnamon

Brew Specs

Method

Shaken Espresso

Espresso

Double Shot

Espresso Beans

18g

Filtered Water (Shot)

36ml

Oat Milk

150ml, cold

Brown Sugar Syrup

2 pumps

Cinnamon

A pinch

Ice

1 cup

Brew Time

8 min

Brew Recipe

01
Pull the EspressoExtract a fresh 36ml double shot into a small cup or shaker. Use immediately while hot.
02
Add Brown Sugar SyrupPour 2 pumps of brown sugar syrup into the hot espresso and stir until fully dissolved.
03
Shake with IceAdd a few ice cubes to the shaker with the espresso mixture and shake vigorously for 10–15 seconds until frothy and chilled.
04
Prepare the GlassFill a tall glass with fresh ice, then pour 150ml of cold oat milk over it.
05
Pour and FinishStrain the shaken espresso over the oat milk, dust lightly with cinnamon, and serve immediately.
