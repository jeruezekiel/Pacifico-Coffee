About Pacifico

A simple project that shares easy coffee recipes you can make at home.

Coffee as Peace in a cup

Pacifico Coffee is a coffee recipe project built around the idea of peace. The name "Pacifico" reflects our goal to keep coffee simple, calm, and easy to enjoy without rushing. We see coffee not just as a drink, but as a small part of the day where things can slow down.

We share easy coffee recipes you can make at home, from simple drinks to café-style favorites. Each recipe has clear steps and balanced flavors, so it's easy for anyone to follow.

More than just recipes, Pacifico Coffee is about the experience of making coffee. We focus on the quiet side of it—the routine, the small details, and the time you take for yourself.

Created by Jeru, Pacifico Coffee reflects the meaning of peace not just in its name, but in the way it presents coffee.
